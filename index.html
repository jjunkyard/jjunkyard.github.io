We are a diverse team of tinkerers, engineers and hobbyists trying to tackle the problems and challenges associated with collaborative robotic systems in space.

Our team briefly competed in the NASA Space Robotics Challenge Phase II which focused on finding solutions to allow a heterogeneous, multi-robot team to autonomously complete tasks envisioned for a lunar in-situ resource utilization (ISRU) mission.

For updates regarding our team's progress and work, have a peek at our blog. Visit our GitHub page for our open-source projects, we are always looking for collaborator, and new pull requests are more than welcome.
